Our Story

A Family Business
Built on Joy

Welcome to Enzo's Ices! We're a passionate family-run ice cream van business that has been bringing smiles to faces across the community for over a decade.

From classic Mr Whippy soft serves to premium Italian gelato, colourful lollies and refreshing slushies — we've got something for everyone, young and old alike!

Our van is fully equipped, immaculately maintained, and always stocked with a fantastic range of frozen treats. We take pride in serving the very best quality ices at every event we attend.

Soft Serve

  • Rainbow Sprinkles

Luxury Gelato

Rich, smooth Italian-style gelato in a rotating selection of indulgent flavours — the real showstopper!

  • Pistachio
  • Salted Caramel
  • Stracciatella
  • Seasonal Specials

Lollies & Treats

  • Calippo Orange

  • Mini Milk (kids)

Slushies

  • Tropical Mix
