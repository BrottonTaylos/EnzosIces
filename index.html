Our Story

For Those Who
Prefer the Best!

Welcome to Enzo's Ices — your local, family-run Mr. Whippy ice cream van serving delicious ice cream, lollies, drinks and sweets to the residents of Ingleby Barwick and beyond since 2013!

From classic freshly whipped soft ice cream cones to indulgent knickerbockers, loaded sharing trays and a huge range of lollies, we've got something to put a smile on every face, young and old alike.

Our iconic pink van (look out for the number plate — ENZ 133!) is a familiar and much-loved sight in Ingleby Barwick and the surrounding area, and we're always delighted to be part of your special occasion.
