Our Story

For Those Who
Prefer the Best!

Welcome to Enzo's Ices — your local, family-run Mr. Whippy ice cream van that's been bringing fresh, delicious treats right to your streets since 2013!

From classic freshly whipped soft ice cream cones to indulgent knickerbockers, loaded sharing trays and a huge range of lollies, we've got something to put a smile on every face, young and old alike.

Our iconic pink van (you'll recognise it by the number plate — ENZ 133!) has visited hundreds of events across the local area and we're always delighted to be part of your special occasion.
